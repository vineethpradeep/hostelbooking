About Karthick PG

Karthick PG has been providing quality accommodations to students and working professionals since 2015. Our mission is to create a home-like environment where residents can focus on their studies or careers without worrying about daily necessities. Located in prime areas of the city, our properties offer easy access to educational institutions, IT parks, and public transportation. We pride ourselves on maintaining clean, safe, and comfortable living spaces with all modern amenities. With over 200 happy residents and a dedicated management team, we ensure that every aspect of your stay is taken care of. From timely maintenance to quality food, we strive to exceed your expectations.
